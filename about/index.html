About

Claudia Vargas

Full Stack Web Developer with experience in UX

Hi there, I'm a citizen of the world who loves traveling, dancing and visual arts. I'm a transplant from Venezuela, lived in France for 6 years and just moved to Providence.

My fascination to understand human behavior drove me to become a Psychologist. However, during my practice, I always felt that something was missing... This "something" was my interest for technology.

I attended a Providence Hackathon in November 2016 and it changed my life. I thought I would not be capable of coding, but I met some developers who encouraged me to try it and I found that I love it!

Since then, I have worked in the development of some web applications. Through those experiences, I learned how to apply and combine UX principles with programming skills and reinforced the ability to perceive and be empathic towards human nature to develop effective programs.

Currently, I am looking for a job as a Web Developer. I would like to work in a company where I can continue to grow up as professional and contribute with my diverse skills to the team. If you're interested in working together or want to talk about Programming, please contact me. I'll be happy to hear from you!
