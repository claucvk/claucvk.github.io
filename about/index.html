More About me and this website

I am from Venezuela and I live in France since 2010. Currently, I'm a UX Researcher & Designer, but in the last 9 years I have been Psychologist. Thanks to my background, I developed some valuable skills about human psychology, social research, technology-design and the interaction between them.
My Masterâ€™s dissertation intertwined the digital world with the child developmental psychology. From the scientific literature I discovered several topics related to Human Computer Interaction and UXD. Gradually, I began to understand what this field was about. The more I got into it the more passionate I became about it. I knew that I had found my dream job. UX Design is a mix of logic, critical thinking, creativity and emotion which I find really AMAZING!

In order to become a UX specialist I'm learning as a self taught and working hard in some projects. I have met professionals in HCI and UXD, consulted forums or websites, made online courses (Coursera and Interaction Design Foundation) and read several books.

In this website you are going to find a growing portafolio of my work and a blog intended to help people to start up in UX Design.

I hope you will find useful or interesting the information in here. If you have any comments, suggestions or just want to talk about any topic don't hesitate to contact me.
