More About me and this website

I am from Venezuela and I live in France since 2010. Currently, I'm a UX Researcher & Designer, but in the last 9 years I have been Psychologist. Thanks to my background, I developed some valuable skills about human psychology, social research, technology-design and the interaction between them.

UX Design is my dream job. It is a mix of logic, critical thinking, creativity and emotion which I find really AMAZING!
At work, I keep a balance between the enterprise's requirements and the user's needs in order to create effective and enjoyable user experiences. The user centered design approach guide my practice. I define the product or service requirements based on an iterative design process: previous research, definition of objectifs, ideation, prototyping and evaluation.

About this website you are going to find a portafolio of my work and a blog intended to help people to start up in UX Design. I hope you will find useful or interesting the information in here. If you have any comments, suggestions or just want to talk about any topic don't hesitate to contact me
