Check some of my works!

When I participate in the design of a service or product I try to keep the balance between the users’ needs and the enterprise’s objectives to reach a design of good quality. It means that all my works are based not only on good principles of aesthetics and functionality aspects, but also in a solid strategy.

UX internship

I was working on tasks related to different aspects of UX Design, UX Research, and Service Design applied to digital and physical spaces.

Some of the tasks that I have done:
1) Identification of target users. 2) Definition of Task Analysis. 3) Conduction of Direct Observation and Interviews. 4) Creation of Personas and User Journey Maps. 5) Creation of Prototypes, sketches, and wireframes: Justinmind, Sketch, POP, and Pencil. 6) Conduction of user Testing. 7) Execution of Usability Expert review. 8) Participation of UX Design workshops (kick-off, ideation, generation, etc.). 9) Participation in "The Global GovJam 2016". 10) Application of the Design Thinking approach.

MKCHROMECAST

This is a program to cast your MacOS audio or Linux audio to Google Cast devices.

Mkchomecast's goal was to create an interface that would be easy and simple to use. The idea was to offer default settings addressed to novice users, but being flexible enough to allow expert users to configure the program with other advanced features, but be flexible enough to allow expert users who know about music, configure the program with others advance features.
