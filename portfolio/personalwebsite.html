Personal Website

User Interface Design (UI)

Goals

The objective was to create a personal website to inform potential employers about my client's professional background.

Tools

In order to reach this goal, I proposed a static website based on Jekyll. A clear Information Architecture (IA) was defined to make the content easy to find and understand. The design was based on the natural science field with a personal touch that characterized my client (colors combination red and black).

Results

The content of the website was divided into 5 main categories. The users can easily find them by accessing two menus, one placed on the top and the other placed on the left side. The definition of these categories, their titles, and the information clustered in each one were aligned to my client's goal. Highlight the key information about her professional background to attract attention from potential employers.
