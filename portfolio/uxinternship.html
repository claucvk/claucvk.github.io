UX Internship

UX Design / UX Research

Goals

TDuring my internship at WD - Human Centered Design, I worked on tasks related to different aspects of UX Design and UX Research applied to digital and physical spaces.

Tools

Some of these tasks were:

  1. Identification of target users.
  2. Definition of Task Analysis.
  3. Conduction of Direct Observation and Interviews.
  4. Creation of Personas and User Journey Maps.
  5. Creation of Prototypes, sketches, and wireframes: Justinmind, Sketch, POP, and Pencil.
  6. Conduction of user Testing.
  7. Execution of Usability Expert review.
  8. Participation of UX Design workshops (kick-off, ideation, generation, etc.).
  9. Participation in "The Global GovJam 2016".
  10. Application of the Design Thinking approach.
