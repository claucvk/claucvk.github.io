Claudia Vargas El Khatib

Web Development Immersive Student at General Assembly

I'm a transplant from Venezuela, living in Providence for 9 months. My background is in Developmental Psychology and I have a little experience in UX. I have always been curious about coding and tech in general. I attended a Providence Hackathon in 2016 and it changed my life. I thought I would not be capable of coding, but I met some developers who encouraged me to try it and I found that I love it!

Currently, I am actively enrolled in General Assembly's Web Development Immersive. The program is very challenging, but I feel very confident that I am on the way to become a full-stack web developer. After graduating, I would like to find a job where I can contribute with my diverse skills to create great user experiences in innovating products.

Specialties: HTML, CSS, JavaScript, Ruby on rails, Git/GitHub, UX, User Centered Design, Sketching, Wireframe, Prototyping.
